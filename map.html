Nestled amidst the vibrant surroundings of Dubai Marina, Royal Regency is perfectly positioned to offer you easy access to the city's key attractions. Just steps away, you'll find the Marina Mall, Marina Walk, and Golf Lovers, Welcome to Royal Regency Suites.

Royal Regency Suites is right next to Top Golf Dubai and Emirates Golf Club. So, if you love golf or just want to try it out, you're in the right place. Take a break from the busy city and enjoy a day on the green.

Explore Dubai from Royal Regency Suites

Our Apartments are just 10 minutes from Jumeirah Beach Residences (JBR) and very close to Marina Walk. This means you can easily explore the best of Dubai. We're near the best spots, and it's easy to get around with metro and tram stations close by. Whether you're here for work or fun, think of us as your peaceful Dubai base.

Relax and Enjoy Local Events at Royal Regency Suites.

When you stay with us, you can enjoy peaceful walks by the sea on the beautifully designed streets of JBR or check out the amazing buildings on Marina Walk. There's always something to do, from skydiving at Sky Dive Dubai to watching the New Year Fireworks or shopping at the annual JBR Dubai Shopping Festival.

Enjoy the Sun at JBR Beach

JBR Beach is a great place to enjoy the Dubai sun and try out different water sports. And the best part? It's right on the doorstep of Royal Regency Suites.
