Four Points by Sheraton Production City, Dubai

The hotel is made up of two buildings, comprises a total of 239 contemporary and deluxe rooms, as well as, junior and one-bedroom suites, and provides convenient solutions for all travelers with exceptional guest service.Close to Dubai’s business areas such as Dubai Media City, Internet City, and JAFZA, and touristic areas such as Jumeirah Beach and Dubai Marina, the hotel has the perfect location to suit the needs of both business and leisure travelers. At the hotel, guests can enjoy a swim in the outdoor pool, workout at the gym, and enjoy relaxing and rejuvenating treatments at the spa. Five restaurants and lounge bars are available to sample the flavors of contemporary and specialty cuisines. At night, live bands and local popular singers pump up the ambiance with favorite music and songs.The hotel caters to the guests’ business needs with well-equipped business centre and 4 meeting rooms, a flexible ballroom, tailored menus and free outdoor and indoor parking.
