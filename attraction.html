Exciting nearby attractions

WELCOME TO ROYAL REGENCY SUITES, WHERE WE GUARANTEE AN EXCEPTIONAL STAY THAT WILL LEAVE YOU WITH UNFORGETTABLE MEMORIES OF DUBAI.

Located in the prestigious Dubai Marina, our hotel offers a prime location with easy access to a wealth of exciting nearby attractions. Here are some popular destinations and their approximate distances from Royal Regency Suites:

The Dubai Mall

Royal Regency Suites, Dubai, offers convenient proximity to The Dubai Mall, the world's largest shopping destination. With an impressive selection of over 1,200 retail outlets, an indoor theme park, an ice rink, and an awe-inspiring aquarium, The Dubai Mall promises an unforgettable experience. Situated just a short drive away, guests can easily access this retail paradise. A mere 20-minute drive from Royal Regency Suites, Dubai, The Dubai Mall invites you to immerse yourself in its unparalleled offerings and create cherished memories.

Dubai Marina Mall

Dubai Marina Mall is an indoor shopping mall in Dubai Marina within 5 minutes walking distance from Royal Regency Suites. It is named for its close proximity to Dubai Marina. The large shopping facility has four levels and 390,000 square feet. There are 140 stores, 21 dining options and a Children's play area. It is one of the main shopping malls in Dubai

Dubai Marina

Dubai Marina, renowned for its captivating waterfront allure and breathtaking cityscape vistas, is a highly sought-after destination. Delight in leisurely walks along the marina promenade, unwind at an array of charming restaurants and cafes, or embark on a scenic boat tour to discover the surrounding areas. Royal Regency Suites, Dubai Marina, offers easy access to Dubai Marina, conveniently located just a short walk from this vibrant hub.

Jumeirah Beach (JBR)

Nestled along the shoreline, Jumeirah Beach offers a serene escape where you can bask in the glorious sun and revel in the pristine sands and shimmering waters. This idyllic beach, celebrated for its crystal-clear waters and soft white sand, is conveniently situated just a short drive away from Royal Regency Suites, Dubai Marina. Guests can easily reach the renowned Jumeirah Beach within a leisurely 20-minute drive, ensuring a delightful retreat to unwind and embrace the beauty of the coastline.

Palm Jumeirah

The tree-shaped Palm Jumeirah island is known for glitzy hotels, posh apartment towers and upmarket global restaurants. Food trucks offering snacks like shawarma dot the Palm Jumeirah Boardwalk, popular for its views of the Dubai coastline and the sail-shaped Burj Al Arab hotel. Beach clubs with spas and infinity pools turn into boisterous nightclubs with live DJs in the evening.

Dubai Eye

This place is the world's tallest and largest observation wheel, with a height of 250 m in Blue Waters Island.

Mall of the Emirates

Situated in close proximity to Royal Regency Suites, Dubai Marina, the Mall of the Emirates stands as a premier shopping haven in Dubai. Just a short distance away, it entices visitors with its expansive collection of upscale fashion brands, gourmet dining options, a state-of-the-art cinema, and the renowned indoor ski slope. Royal Regency Suites, Dubai Marina, offers convenient access to this vibrant retail destination, located a mere 10-minute drive away.
